A man of courage, brains, and heart

Growing up in rural Southwestern Pennsylvania between a farm and his family’s resort in the Appalachian Mountains; Nemacolin Woodlands, PJ chose to begin telling some of his earliest relocations of his childhood through the lens of his favorite movie, Wizard of Oz. This contrast between reality and fantasy emulates Dorothy’s journey from Kansas to Oz. Throughout Dorothy’s journey, her goal was to defeat the Wicked Witch of the West. PJ takes quite the opposite approach in this film. In fact, he embraces with Wicked Witch wholeheartedly.
