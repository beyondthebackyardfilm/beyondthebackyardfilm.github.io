Through all the valleys and all the hills

The film has strong themes of PJ’s fascination with powerful female figures like his mother. His mother’s strong presence in his life has influenced a fascination and admiration for the Wicked Witch from a young age. His mother is played by his biological mother, Maggie. PJ’s ninety three year old grandfather, Joe Hardy, plays the wizard himself, soaring high into the sky in a hot air balloon. This is something that his grandfather used to do quite often. Mr. Hardy fits the wizard archetype due to his entrepreneurial trajectory from a small town man with big dreams. Throughout the film, PJ intercuts VHS footage and old family photos to aide in the justification of the characters in the film.
